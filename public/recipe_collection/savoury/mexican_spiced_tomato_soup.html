Mexican Spiced Tomato Soup¶

from Kerry Cane
Serves: A lot
Time: 20 mins prep time, 40 mins cook time
Equipment: Knife, chopping board, large pot, and a way to boil water for stock

Ingredients¶

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • 2 onions, finely chopped

  • 1 cup celery, finely chopped

  • 2 carrots, finely chopped

  • 3 gloves garlic

  • 2 tsp ground cumin

  • 1 tsp chili powder

  • 1 tsp oregano

  • 2 tsp sugar

  • 1/2 tsp salt

  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes,

  • 6 cups beef stock

  • 1/3 cup tomato paste

  • 1 1/2 cups red lentils

  • 2 tbsp brown sugar

  • Optional - Italian pork sausages, 1 per person (Split the skins and squeeze out the meat. Crumble into small pieces and fry in a pan.)

Directions¶

  1. Sauté onions, celery, carrots and garlic in oil until soft.

  2. Combine cumin, chili, oregano, sugar and salt and stir into pot.

  3. Add tomatoes, stock, tomato paste, lentils and brown sugar.

  4. Bring to the boil and stir, then reduce the temperature and simmer for 40 minutes.

  5. Garnish with chopped parsley.

Variations¶

Replace beef stock with vege stock and don’t use pork sausages for a fully vegetarian dish
