Kumara and Bacon Soup¶

Serves: 4
Time:
Equipment:

Ingredients¶

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • 2 tbsp butter

  • 2 large onions, peeled and diced

  • 6 rashers rindless bacon, diced

  • 4 large kumara (sweet potatoes)

  • 6 cups chicken or vegetable stock

  • ¼ cup sour cream

  • salt and pepper

  • snipped chives

Directions¶

  1. In a large saucepan sweat onions in oil and butter until softened but not coloured then remove from pan. Add bacon and gently fry until cooked through and a little crispy. Set aside a few tablespoons of the bacon as a garnish.

  2. Place onions, bacon, kumara and stock into saucepan and bring to the boil. Simmer gently until kumara is very tender. Add sour cream and season with salt and pepper.

  3. Purée in a blender or food processor. Return to pan to heat through. Adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve topped with extra bacon and snipped chives.
