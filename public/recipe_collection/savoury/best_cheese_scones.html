Best Cheese Scones¶

from Kerry Cane
Serves: Approx 8 scones
Time: 10 mins prep time, 10-15 mins cook time
Equipment: Mixing bowl, grater, baking tray, oven

Ingredients¶

  • 2 cups self raising flour,

  • 2 cups grated cheese, plus extra for sprinkling (1 use a mixture of Colby and Parmesan)

  • pinch salt

  • 1 1/4 cups milk

Directions¶

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C

  2. Sift together flour and cheese.

  3. Add milk and mix until just combined. Shape, place on a tray and sprinkle with extra cheese. (I sometimes put a dollop of chutney or pesto on top as well).

  4. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes until well risen and crusty.

Variations¶

If you don’t have self-raising flour, use 1 1/2 tsp of baking and a pinch of salt for every 1 cup of plain flour.
