========
Oat Bars
========
| 
| **Serves**: Approx 10-12 bars
| **Time**: 10 mins prep time, 25 minutes cook time
| **Equipment**: Mixing bowl, food processor or blender, baking dish, oven

-----------
Ingredients
-----------
* 2 1/2 cups rolled oats (fine)
* 1/4 cup honey
* 1/4 cup of light oil (e.g. canola or sunflower oil)
* 1/8 cup maple syrup
* 1 tsp vanilla essence
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp baking soda

----------
Directions
----------
#. Preheat oven to 175C
#. Blend 1/2 cup of rolled oats into a flour-like powder
#. In a large bowl, mix together blended oats with honey, oil, maple syrup, and vanilla to make a smooth paste
#. Added remaining oats, salt, and baking soda and mix until thoroughly combined
#. Place mixture into a lined baking dish, and press down firmly
#. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden
#. Immediately on removing the dish from the oven, use the bottom of a glass or another tray to press down firmly on the baked oats
#. Leave to cool completely before removing from the baking dish and slicing into bars

----------
Variations
----------
Swap maple syrup for 2 heaped tablespoons of honey for peanutty goodness.
Add a sprinkle of cinnamon for a warm spicy taste.
