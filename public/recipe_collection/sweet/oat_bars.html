Oat Bars¶


Serves: Approx 10-12 bars
Time: 10 mins prep time, 25 minutes cook time
Equipment: Mixing bowl, food processor or blender, baking dish, oven

Ingredients¶

  • 2 1/2 cups rolled oats (fine)

  • 1/4 cup honey

  • 1/4 cup of light oil (e.g. canola or sunflower oil)

  • 1/8 cup maple syrup

  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

  • 1 tsp salt

  • 1/2 tsp baking soda

Directions¶

  1. Preheat oven to 175C

  2. Blend 1/2 cup of rolled oats into a flour-like powder

  3. In a large bowl, mix together blended oats with honey, oil, maple syrup, and vanilla to make a smooth paste

  4. Added remaining oats, salt, and baking soda and mix until thoroughly combined

  5. Place mixture into a lined baking dish, and press down firmly

  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden

  7. Immediately on removing the dish from the oven, use the bottom of a glass or another tray to press down firmly on the baked oats

  8. Leave to cool completely before removing from the baking dish and slicing into bars

Variations¶

Swap maple syrup for 2 heaped tablespoons of honey for peanutty goodness. Add a sprinkle of cinnamon for a warm spicy taste.
