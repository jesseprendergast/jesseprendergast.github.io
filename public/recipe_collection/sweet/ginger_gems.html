Ginger gems (Nana’s recipe)¶

Serves:
Time:
Equipment:

Ingredients¶

  • 1 ½ cups flour

  • ¾ cup sugar

  • ¾ cup milk

  • 1 tbsp golden syrup

  • 1 level tsp baking soda

  • 1 egg

  • 2 level tsp ground ginger

  • 50g butter

Directions¶

  1. Preheat oven to 180 with gem irons in oven.

  2. Cream butter and sugar. Beat in egg and golden syrup.

  3. Dissolve soda in milk.

  4. Add to butter mixture all dry ingredients and soda/milk.

  5. Put into hot gem irons and bake in oven for 10 mins.
